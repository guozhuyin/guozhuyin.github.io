Nianbada Childhood School

2019 -

Hsinchu, TW

  • Treetopia: creating a 1970s place of play
Back in the 1970s, Taiwanese children used to have lively childhoods involving outdoor games, such as street games and playground games. Over time, these games have gradually disappeared. Children today have very little time to play and few places to play in. As a result, children today have less of a childhood compared to children of the past. To address this crisis, we built Treetopia in Zhubei, Taiwan, in 2011. Treetopia is an open park designed to let kids play outdoors. We teach Taiwanese culture and 1970s outdoor games to encourage children to lead more active, lively and fulfilling childhoods.
